Brief CV

About me

I received the B.Eng. degree in mechanical engineering from Southwest Jiaotong University, Chengdu, China, in 2016, and the M.Eng. degree in mechanical engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China, in 2019. I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering with the Department of Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong. My research interests include fault diagnosis, signal processing, and deep learning. Recently, I am focusing on domain generalization-based fault diagnosis and vibrational signal denoising.
