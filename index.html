About Me

I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from City University of Hong Kong in 2024. I also hold an M.Eng. from Xi'an Jiaotong University (2019) and a B.Eng. from Southwest Jiaotong University (2016). My research focuses on fault diagnosis and transfer learning. Following my Ph.D., I worked as a Researcher at Huawei Hong Kong Research Center (Huawei HKRC). Since April 2025, I have been an Algorithm Expert at Alibaba Group, where I focus on LLM training, specifically LLM architecture design and training acceleration.
